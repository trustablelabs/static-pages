Tool Categories Explained

The GEO tools market has evolved into three distinct categories. Understanding these categories is essential for choosing the right tool.

Monitoring-Only Tools

These platforms track how your brand appears in AI responses. They answer: "Where do we stand?" Key metrics include citation frequency, sentiment, competitive share, and query coverage. They do NOT provide optimization recommendations or content guidance.

Examples: Otterly, Profound, Searchable, AthenaHQ, Scrunch, Promptmonitor, Knowatoa

Best for: Brands that already have content/SEO teams and just need visibility data.

Optimization Tools

These platforms help improve content for AI discoverability. They answer: "How do we improve?" They provide content recommendations, AI-friendly formatting guidance, and sometimes AI content generation. They typically do NOT provide comprehensive visibility monitoring.

Examples: Peec AI, Writesonic, ZipTie, Rankscale

Best for: Content teams focused on creation and optimization.

Full-Loop Platforms

These combine monitoring with optimization recommendations. They answer both "Where do we stand?" and "How do we improve?" This category is newer and less mature but addresses the gap between knowing your visibility and improving it.

Examples: Goodie, Trustable

Best for: Teams wanting an integrated approach without using multiple tools.

For a deeper analysis of this distinction, see Monitoring vs Optimization in GEO.

Monitoring-Only Tools: Detailed Breakdown

Otterly.ai

From $99/month · Agency plans from $299/month

ChatGPT · Perplexity · Claude · Gemini

Otterly has emerged as the leading GEO monitoring platform, particularly for agencies. Key strengths include the broadest LLM coverage (4 major models), multi-brand management, white-label reporting, and a relatively fast onboarding process. Limitations include no optimization recommendations and API access only on higher tiers.

Key differentiator: Best multi-brand/agency features in the market.

Profound

From $149/month · Enterprise custom pricing

ChatGPT · Perplexity · Gemini

Profound positions as an enterprise-grade analytics platform. Strengths include deep reporting, native CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot), custom report builders, and dedicated customer success for enterprise. Limitations include higher pricing, no Claude tracking, and steeper onboarding.

Key differentiator: Deepest analytics and enterprise integrations.

Searchable

From $79/month

ChatGPT · Perplexity · Claude

Searchable offers core citation tracking at a lower price point. The platform emphasizes simplicity and fast time-to-value. Notable for strong Claude coverage where some competitors lack it. Less suitable for enterprise needs or complex multi-brand scenarios.

Key differentiator: Best value for straightforward monitoring.

AthenaHQ

From $129/month

ChatGPT · Perplexity · Gemini · Google AI Overviews

AthenaHQ focuses heavily on Google AI Overviews alongside traditional LLM monitoring. Useful for brands where Google's AI features are a primary concern. The AI Overviews tracking is a differentiator many competitors don't offer.

Key differentiator: Strong Google AI Overviews coverage.

Scrunch

From $69/month

ChatGPT · Perplexity

Scrunch offers budget-friendly monitoring with limited engine coverage. Suitable for small teams testing the GEO monitoring concept before committing to larger platforms. Trade-off is fewer features and narrower LLM coverage.

Key differentiator: Lowest-cost dedicated GEO monitoring.

Promptmonitor

From $89/month

ChatGPT · Perplexity · Claude

Promptmonitor takes a developer-focused approach with strong API access and integration capabilities. More technical than competitors, suitable for teams wanting to build custom dashboards or integrate monitoring into existing systems.

Knowatoa

Free tier available · Paid from $29/month

ChatGPT · Perplexity

Knowatoa's free tier makes it the most accessible entry point for GEO monitoring. Limited query volume and features, but allows testing the concept without commitment. Good for understanding baseline visibility before investing in paid tools.

Which Tool for Which Use Case

For Agencies Managing Multiple Brands

Best choice: Otterly.ai — Purpose-built for multi-brand management with white-label reporting. Profound works for enterprise agencies willing to pay more for deeper analytics.

For Enterprise Single-Brand

Best choice: Profound — Deepest analytics, CRM integrations, dedicated support. Consider AthenaHQ if Google AI Overviews are critical.

For Startups/SMBs on Budget

Best choice: Searchable or Trustable — Searchable offers solid monitoring at $79/mo. Trustable's free tier allows testing before commitment.

For Content Teams

Best choice: Peec AI — Focus on content optimization rather than just monitoring. Combine with a monitoring tool for complete coverage.

For Technical/Developer Teams

Best choice: Promptmonitor — API-first design, integration-friendly, developer documentation.

For Google AI Overviews Focus

Best choice: ZipTie or AthenaHQ — Specialized coverage of Google's AI features that many tools don't track.

For Testing/Exploration

Best choice: Knowatoa or Trustable free tier — Understand your baseline before committing budget.

Conclusion

The GEO tools market is maturing rapidly. The key decision is whether you need monitoring only, optimization only, or both. Most mature organizations will eventually need both capabilities — either through integrated platforms or combining specialized tools.

For most teams, we recommend:

  1. Start with free tiers to understand baseline visibility
  2. Choose based on primary need — monitoring vs. optimization
  3. Consider engine coverage — ensure tools track the AI systems your audience uses
  4. Plan for both capabilities — monitoring without optimization leaves half the job undone
