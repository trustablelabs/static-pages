Quick Answer: Otterly.ai is better for agencies and multi-brand monitoring with broader LLM coverage and more accessible pricing. Profound is better for enterprise teams needing deep analytics, CRM integrations, and custom reporting. Both are monitoring-focused tools — neither provides optimization recommendations. For teams under $150/mo budget or managing multiple brands, choose Otterly. For enterprise brands needing integration with existing marketing stacks, choose Profound.

Overview

Otterly.ai and Profound are the two most-discussed dedicated GEO platforms in 2026. Both focus on monitoring brand visibility in AI language models, but they target different market segments and prioritize different features.

Otterly.ai: The Agency Favorite

Otterly.ai emerged as a purpose-built GEO monitoring tool and has become particularly popular with agencies managing multiple client brands. The platform emphasizes breadth of LLM coverage and ease of multi-brand management.

Key Strengths

  • Broadest LLM coverage — Tracks ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini in one dashboard
  • Multi-brand architecture — Designed for agencies managing 10+ brands
  • White-label options — Agency plans allow branded reports for clients
  • Lower entry point — $99/mo gets you started vs Profound's $149/mo
  • Faster onboarding — Most users report going live in under an hour

Limitations

  • Less deep analytics — Dashboards are more surface-level than Profound
  • Limited integrations — No native CRM or marketing stack connections
  • API on higher tiers only — Programmatic access requires more expensive plans
  • No optimization guidance — Monitoring only, no recommendations

Pricing

  • Starter: $99/mo — Single brand, 50 queries, weekly monitoring
  • Professional: $199/mo — 3 brands, 200 queries, daily monitoring
  • Agency: $499/mo — 10 brands, 500 queries, API access, white-label
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing

Profound: The Enterprise Choice

Profound positions itself as an enterprise-grade AI visibility analytics platform. The focus is on deep insights, integration with existing marketing technology stacks, and enterprise-level support.

Key Strengths

  • Deeper analytics — More granular data and trend analysis
  • CRM integrations — Native Salesforce, HubSpot, and Marketo connections
  • Custom reporting — Build reports matching your internal needs
  • API on all paid plans — Programmatic access without premium pricing
  • Dedicated support — Enterprise customers get dedicated CSMs
  • Data exports — Easy export for custom analysis

Limitations

  • Narrower LLM coverage — Doesn't track Claude (yet)
  • Higher entry price — $149/mo minimum
  • Longer onboarding — More complex setup, especially with integrations
  • Less agency-friendly — Multi-brand features are enterprise tier
  • No optimization guidance — Monitoring only, like Otterly

Pricing

  • Professional: $149/mo — Single brand, 100 queries, API access
  • Business: $349/mo — 3 brands, 300 queries, CRM integrations
  • Enterprise: Custom — Unlimited brands, custom integrations, dedicated CSM

Head-to-Head Comparison

LLM Coverage

Winner: Otterly — Tracks 4 LLMs vs Profound's 3. Claude coverage is notable given Anthropic's growing market share.

Ease of Use

Winner: Otterly — Simpler interface, faster onboarding. Profound has more power but steeper learning curve.

Analytics Depth

Winner: Profound — More granular data, better trend analysis, custom reporting capabilities.

Integrations

Winner: Profound — Native CRM connections vs Otterly's standalone approach.

Multi-Brand Management

Winner: Otterly — Purpose-built for agencies. Profound charges enterprise pricing for comparable features.

Value for Money

Depends: Otterly offers more for less at entry level. Profound's value appears at higher tiers where integrations matter.

The Verdict

Choose Otterly if:

  • You manage multiple brands (agency model)
  • Budget is under $200/mo
  • You need Claude monitoring
  • White-label reporting matters
  • You want fast, simple onboarding

Choose Profound if:

  • You're an enterprise brand (single brand, deep analytics)
  • CRM integration is essential
  • Custom reporting is a requirement
  • You have budget for $300+/mo
  • Dedicated support matters

What Neither Tool Does

Both Otterly and Profound are monitoring platforms. They show you where you stand but don't tell you how to improve. This is a significant gap in the GEO tools market.

For optimization guidance, consider:

  • Pairing either tool with content optimization platforms like Frase or Surfer
  • Using platforms that combine monitoring with optimization (like Trustable)
  • Building internal expertise using GEO research and techniques
