What is a GEO Tool?

A GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) tool helps brands understand and improve how they appear in AI-generated responses. Unlike traditional SEO tools that focus on search engine rankings, GEO tools monitor large language models like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini.

These tools typically provide:

  • Citation tracking — How often is your brand mentioned when users ask relevant questions?
  • Sentiment analysis — Is the AI saying positive, negative, or neutral things about you?
  • Competitor comparison — How does your AI visibility compare to competitors?
  • Query monitoring — What questions trigger mentions of your brand?

The GEO tools market emerged in 2024-2025 as businesses realized that AI assistants were increasingly influencing purchasing decisions. A study from Princeton researchers found that specific content optimization techniques could improve AI visibility by 40-115%.

Detailed Tool Breakdown

Otterly.ai

Otterly.ai has established itself as the most comprehensive GEO monitoring platform, particularly popular with agencies managing multiple brands. The platform tracks mentions across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini with daily or weekly monitoring schedules.

✓ Strengths

  • Broadest LLM coverage
  • Multi-brand dashboard
  • Historical trend tracking
  • API access on higher tiers

✗ Limitations

  • Higher price point
  • Monitoring only, no optimization
  • Learning curve for new users

Pricing: Starts at $99/month for single-brand monitoring. Agency plans with multiple brands start at $299/month. Enterprise pricing available.

Profound

Profound focuses on enterprise-grade AI visibility analytics with deep reporting capabilities. The platform emphasizes data quality and offers integration with existing marketing analytics stacks.

✓ Strengths

  • Enterprise reporting
  • CRM integrations
  • Custom query sets
  • Dedicated support

✗ Limitations

  • Premium pricing
  • Overkill for small teams
  • Longer setup process

Pricing: Starts at $149/month. Enterprise plans with custom integrations available on request.

Searchable

Searchable emerged as a fast-growing alternative focused on simplicity and affordability. The platform raised significant funding in 2025 and offers a streamlined interface for citation tracking.

✓ Strengths

  • Affordable entry point
  • Clean, simple interface
  • Fast onboarding
  • Active development

✗ Limitations

  • Fewer LLMs than competitors
  • Limited historical data
  • Newer platform (less proven)

Pricing: Starts at $79/month. Annual discounts available.

Peec AI

Peec AI takes a content-first approach, focusing on optimizing content for AI discoverability rather than just monitoring. The platform provides recommendations for improving content structure.

Pricing: Starts at $49/month. Free trial available.

Knowatoa

Knowatoa offers a free tier that makes it accessible for testing and exploration. The platform provides basic monitoring for ChatGPT and Perplexity with limited query volume.

Pricing: Free tier with 10 queries/month. Paid plans from $29/month.

Trustable

Trustable differentiates by combining monitoring with optimization guidance. Rather than just showing where you're cited, the platform provides specific recommendations for improving AI visibility based on research-backed GEO techniques.

✓ Strengths

  • Monitoring + optimization combined
  • Research-based recommendations
  • Free tier available
  • Multi-LLM coverage

✗ Limitations

  • Newer to market
  • Smaller user base
  • Enterprise features developing

Pricing: Free tier available. Paid plans from $49/month.

How to Choose the Right GEO Tool

Consider these factors when selecting a GEO tool:

  1. Budget — Free tiers exist (Knowatoa, Trustable) for testing. Serious monitoring starts around $79-149/month.
  2. Scale — Managing one brand vs. multiple brands affects which platform makes sense.
  3. Goals — Do you need monitoring only, or do you want optimization recommendations?
  4. LLM coverage — Ensure the tool tracks the AI systems your audience actually uses.
  5. Integrations — Enterprise teams may need API access or CRM connections.

The Monitoring vs. Optimization Gap

Most GEO tools focus exclusively on monitoring — they tell you where you stand but not how to improve. This is similar to early SEO tools that showed rankings without optimization guidance.

The gap between "knowing your AI visibility" and "improving your AI visibility" remains significant. Some platforms are beginning to address this with recommendations, but the market is still maturing. For more on this distinction, see Monitoring vs Optimization in GEO.
