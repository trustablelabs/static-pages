What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?

How to optimize content for featured snippets, voice search, and direct answer formats.

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing content to appear in direct answer formats — including Google's featured snippets, voice search results, knowledge panels, People Also Ask boxes, and AI-generated answer summaries. AEO focuses on providing clear, concise answers that search engines can extract and display directly to users.

AEO vs SEO vs GEO

Aspect SEO AEO GEO
Primary Target Organic search rankings Featured snippets & direct answers AI system citations
Content Format Long-form, comprehensive Concise, question-focused Structured, authoritative
User Experience Click to website Answer shown in search Answer synthesized by AI
Key Platforms Google, Bing Google, Alexa, Siri ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude

Types of Answer Formats

Featured Snippets

The highlighted box at the top of Google search results that directly answers a query. Types include paragraph snippets, list snippets, table snippets, and video snippets.

Knowledge Panels

Information boxes that appear for entity searches (people, places, organizations). Sourced from Google's Knowledge Graph, Wikipedia, and structured data.

People Also Ask (PAA)

Expandable question boxes that show related queries. Each expanded answer can cite a different source.

Voice Search Results

The single answer that voice assistants (Google Assistant, Alexa, Siri) read aloud. Usually pulled from featured snippets or top-ranking content.

AI Overviews

Google's AI-generated summaries that synthesize information from multiple sources. Represents the convergence of AEO and GEO.

AEO Optimization Strategies

Content Structure

  • Question-based headings — Use "What is," "How to," "Why does" formats
  • Concise definitions — Answer the question in 40-60 words immediately after the heading
  • Structured lists — Numbered steps for processes, bullet points for features
  • Tables for comparisons — Google frequently extracts tables for snippets

Schema Markup

  • FAQPage — For frequently asked questions
  • HowTo — For step-by-step guides
  • QAPage — For single question-answer content
  • Speakable — Marks content suitable for voice assistants

Content Quality

  • Authoritative sourcing — Cite credible sources for claims
  • Factual accuracy — Incorrect answers lose snippet position
  • Regular updates — Fresh content maintains snippet eligibility
  • Complete coverage — Address follow-up questions users might have

Measuring AEO Success

  • Featured snippet wins — Track which queries show your content in position zero
  • PAA appearances — Monitor People Also Ask box inclusions
  • Voice search testing — Test queries on voice assistants
  • CTR changes — Featured snippets can increase or decrease clicks depending on query type

AEO and GEO: The Convergence

As AI systems increasingly power search results, AEO and GEO are converging. Google AI Overviews use similar principles to traditional featured snippets but with AI synthesis. Content optimized for AEO often performs well for GEO because both reward:

  • Clear, direct answers to specific questions
  • Well-structured content with semantic markup
  • Authoritative sourcing and factual accuracy
  • Comprehensive coverage of related subtopics
